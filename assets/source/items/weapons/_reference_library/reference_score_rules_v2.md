# Reference Score Rules v2

## Score Weights

- visual_quality: 15
- minecraft_weapon_fit: 20
- minecraft_costume_fit: 20
- silhouette_readability: 15
- weapon_costume_cohesion: 20
- poro_fit: 5
- risk_control: 5

## Grade

- A: 82~100
  Use as primary selected reference.
- B: 65~81
  Use only for specific part or costume/palette logic.
- C: 45~64
  Store as mood reference only.
- D: 0~44
  Reject.

## Good Weapon Reference
- strong silhouette
- readable major parts
- voxel-friendly mass
- not too effect-dependent

## Good Costume Reference
- readable silhouette
- limited palette
- large readable motifs
- clear class identity
- can be simplified into Minecraft wearable pieces

## Good Weapon+Costume Set
- strong theme cohesion
- weapon and outfit feel designed together
- readable at a glance
- suitable for a class/set identity

## Risk Control

style_anchor:

private_homage:

direct_recreation:
