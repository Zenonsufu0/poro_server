# Reference Score Rules v1

## Score Weights

- visual_quality: 20
- minecraft_feasibility: 30
- silhouette_readability: 20
- weapon_costume_cohesion: 15
- poro_fit: 10
- risk_control: 5

## Grade

- A: 80~100
  Use as primary selected reference.
- B: 60~79
  Use only for specific part or palette.
- C: 40~59
  Store as mood reference only.
- D: 0~39
  Reject.

## Minecraft Feasibility

High:
- strong silhouette
- few large readable parts
- clear weapon shape
- no excessive thin curves
- no heavy transparent effects

Low:
- too many small ornaments
- thin ribbons/chains
- effect-dependent design
- silhouette unreadable without color
- complex cloth or hair-like details

## Risk Control

style_anchor:
- General mood, palette, silhouette only.

private_homage:
- Private friends-only server reference.
- Do not publish or sell.

direct_recreation:
- Avoid.
- Keep only for private test.
