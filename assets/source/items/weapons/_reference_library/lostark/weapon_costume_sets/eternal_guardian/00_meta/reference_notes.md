## Selected References

### 03_spear
- lance: A
- Use for spear silhouette, spearhead design, guardian spear palette.
- Moonlight = frost/north spear.
- Starlight = imperial/holy spear.

### 04_shortsword
- reaper dagger: A
- Use for assassin dagger and curved short blade silhouette.

### 05_doublesword
- blade dualsword: A
- Use for assassin dual-blade silhouette and moon/gold palette variants.

### 06_gun

### 07_staff
- summoner staff: A-

### 08_orbs
- arcanist deck: A+
- Use as primary orb direction.
- Orb should be an outer magical frame/card/ring device, not just a simple ball.

### 09_arks
- qigong core: A
- demonic weapon: A-
- Use as core/artifact/magic nucleus direction.
- Ark/Core should be a central energy source or relic, distinct from orb frames.
